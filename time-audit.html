4 — Automation Opportunities Draft

Ranked by: (hours reclaimed/week) × (directness to quota goal). Based on stated pain points in CLAUDE.md, not derived from work system data.

#1 — Highest Impact
Meeting recaps write themselves and land in Salesforce before your next call starts.
Quick Win ~3–4 hrs/week reclaimed
What's happening today
After every call, you manually reconstruct what was discussed, type a recap, and log it to Salesforce. At ~20 min/call × 10+ calls/week, this is 3+ hours of post-call admin weekly.
Automated version
Trigger: Zoom/Google Meet call ends. AI transcribes + summarizes key points, next steps, and deal signals. Formats as Salesforce activity log. You review and approve in 30 seconds — or set it to auto-log.
#2
You walk into every call already knowing the account, the stakeholder, and the right angle — without spending time on it.
Quick Win ~2–3 hrs/week reclaimed
What's happening today
Before each call you manually look up the account in Salesforce, search LinkedIn for the contact, check recent news, and assemble context. ~15–20 min per meeting.
Automated version
Trigger: Meeting appears on work calendar. AI pulls Salesforce account data, LinkedIn profile, recent news/press releases, and prior call notes. Delivers a 1-page brief to your inbox 30 min before the call.
#3
Outreach drafts exist in your queue before you've thought about writing them.
Medium Lift ~2 hrs/week reclaimed
What's happening today
Prospecting emails are written from scratch. Each first-touch email requires researching the account, finding a relevant angle, and drafting copy — ~15–20 min per prospect.
Automated version
Trigger: New account added to Salesforce or moved to "Target" stage. AI researches the account + contact, identifies a relevant pricing/transparency angle, and drafts a personalized first-touch email. You edit and send — no blank page.
#4
Follow-ups never fall through the cracks — they surface automatically based on deal stage and time since last activity.
Medium Lift ~1–2 hrs/week reclaimed
What's happening today
You mentally track who you haven't followed up with. Deals go quiet because there's no automated nudge — just your memory and a crowded calendar.
Automated version
Trigger: Salesforce detects no activity on an open opportunity for X days. AI generates a follow-up draft contextual to the deal stage and last interaction. Delivered to your inbox as a ready-to-send draft.

Quick Win ~1 hr/week reclaimed
