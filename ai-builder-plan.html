What I'm Building
01
Meeting recaps write themselves and land in Salesforce before the next call starts.
Quick Win ~3–4 hrs / week Not Started
What's happening today
After every call, manually reconstruct what was discussed, type a recap, and log it to Salesforce. At ~20 min/call × 10+ calls/week, this is 3+ hours of post-call admin every single week.
Automated version
Trigger: Zoom/Meet call ends. AI transcribes and summarizes key points, next steps, and deal signals. Formats as a Salesforce activity log. Review and approve in 30 seconds — or set to auto-log.
02
Walk into every call already knowing the account, the stakeholder, and the right angle.
Quick Win ~2–3 hrs / week Not Started
What's happening today
Before each call: manually look up the account in Salesforce, search LinkedIn for the contact, check recent news, assemble context. ~15–20 min per meeting — every meeting.
Automated version
Trigger: Meeting appears on work calendar. AI pulls Salesforce account data, LinkedIn profile, recent news and prior call notes. Delivers a 1-page brief to inbox 30 min before the call.
03
Outreach drafts exist in the queue before you've thought about writing them.
Medium Lift ~2 hrs / week Not Started
What's happening today
Prospecting emails written from scratch. Each first-touch requires researching the account, finding a pricing/transparency angle, and drafting copy — ~15–20 min per prospect.
Automated version
Trigger: Account added to Salesforce or moved to "Target" stage. AI researches account + contact, identifies a relevant transparency angle, drafts a personalized first-touch. You edit and send — no blank page.
04
Follow-ups surface automatically — deals never go quiet because you forgot.
Medium Lift ~1–2 hrs / week Not Started
What's happening today
Mentally tracking who hasn't been followed up with. Deals go quiet because there's no automated nudge — just memory and a crowded calendar.
Automated version
Trigger: Salesforce detects no activity on an open opportunity for X days. AI generates a context-aware follow-up draft based on deal stage and last interaction. Delivered as a ready-to-send draft.
05
Healthcare pricing intelligence lands weekly — without hunting for it.
Quick Win ~1 hr / week Not Started
What's happening today
Reading Healthcare Brew and morning newsletters passively. Good intent, but unfocused — not targeted at the specific topics that make a better AE: price transparency enforcement, hospital compliance, CMS updates.
Automated version
Trigger: Weekly schedule. AI scans news, CMS updates, and LinkedIn. Delivers a curated 5–7 item briefing, each tagged with "how to use in a sales conversation." Builds SME rep without adding reading time.
06
Personal admin noise never interrupts a selling day.
Quick Win ~30 min / week Not Started
What's happening today
DocuSign reminders, shipping notifications, financial account nudges, and sports group emails surface during work hours and pull attention — visible in the time audit inbox data.
Automated version
Gmail filter + AI triage: personal admin routed to a "handle later" folder. Daily digest at 7pm surfaces anything that actually needs action. Zero cognitive interruption during selling hours.
